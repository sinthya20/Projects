Chocolate Cake is one of the most popular cakes worldwide. It's made with rich cocoa and dark chocolate, creating a moist and flavorful dessert. Common variations include adding chocolate ganache, frosting, and even layers of chocolate mousse.

Vanilla Cake is a timeless classic. It features a light, fluffy texture with a delicate vanilla flavor. Often topped with buttercream frosting, vanilla cake is perfect for almost any occasion, from birthdays to weddings.

Red Velvet Cake is a unique cake with a deep red color and a subtle chocolate flavor. Traditionally paired with cream cheese frosting, it’s a beautiful cake often served at special events like Valentine's Day or anniversaries.

Carrot Cake is a spiced cake with grated carrots added to the batter. Often combined with cinnamon and walnuts, it’s traditionally topped with cream cheese frosting, giving it a rich flavor and moist texture.

Cheesecake is a decadent dessert made from cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, often with a buttery graham cracker crust. It's incredibly versatile, with various flavor options such as strawberry, blueberry, and chocolate.

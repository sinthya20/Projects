Medha Event at GIET College

A celebration of talent and innovation

About Medha

Medha is an annual event organized by GIET College to showcase the talents and skills of its students.

Students participate in various competitions, workshops, and cultural events, making Medha a vibrant and enriching experience.

For more details, click here.

Highlights of Medha

  • Competitions in coding, design, and public speaking
  • Guest lectures and seminars by industry experts
  • Cultural performances and talent shows

Medha Games and Competitions

Quiz Competition

Test your knowledge with our quiz competition! Answer questions from various topics.

Format: Online quiz

How to Play:

  • Register for the quiz on the event website.
  • Access the quiz link at the designated time.
  • Answer the questions within the given time limit.
  • Top scorers will be awarded prizes.

Treasure Hunting

Join the exciting treasure hunting adventure around the campus!

Format: On-campus exploration

How to Play:

  • Form a team of 2-4 members.
  • Collect clues placed around the campus.
  • Solve puzzles and riddles to find the final treasure.
  • The first team to find the treasure wins.

Ideathon

Bring your innovative ideas to life in our Ideathon competition!

Format: Team presentation

How to Play:

  • Form a team of 2-5 members.
  • Develop a creative and innovative idea.
  • Prepare a presentation explaining your idea.
  • Present your idea to a panel of judges.
  • The winning team will receive a prize.

Poster presentation

Showcase your creativity with our poster design competition!

Format: Individual submission

How to Play:

  • Design a poster based on the given theme.
  • Submit your poster in digital format.
  • Posters will be judged based on creativity, originality, and visual appeal.
  • The winning poster will be displayed at the event.
